Features :
1.An SOS is generated when a patient is struck with an emergent medical condition.
2.This SOS request reaches to the doctor and he can decide if he wishes to attend the patient or not.
3.The request matching is done on the basis of the medical history of the patient and the specialisation of the doctor.
4.(peculiarity)If the doctor is associated with any hospital he/she can send the request for fetching the patient to his own prospective hospital immediately.
